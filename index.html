About Veleris

A personal wallpaper studio.

Veleris is built around a simple idea: your screen should feel as considered as everything around it. Every wallpaper is curated to be clean and modern — designed to sit in the background while still giving your desktop a premium, finished look.

From minimal gradients to abstract pieces, nature scenes and anime-inspired visuals, each collection is crafted to feel cohesive, sharp and tuned for high-resolution displays.

You’re welcome to use these for your personal desktop. If you share them online, a small credit back to Veleris goes a long way.
